# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want this repository to be a welcoming place for first-time contributors, maintainers, and users operating production payment integrations. Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

## Expected behavior

- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus criticism on code, behavior, and decisions, not on people.
- Help newer contributors understand repository expectations.
- Keep technical disagreements factual and actionable.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language
- Dismissing people for asking beginner questions
- Sharing private credentials, customer data, or security-sensitive information in public threads

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this policy. Repeated or severe violations can lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from the project spaces.

## Reporting

If you need to report a problem, open a private security-style report or contact the maintainers through the repository’s security contact path instead of escalating publicly with sensitive details.
